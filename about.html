FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Is this therapy?

No. It is educational and wellness-oriented. It is guided, not clinical, and not a substitute for professional care.

What if we are not in a bad place?

This is designed for couples who want to stay intentional. Not a crisis tool, more like maintenance.

How long does it take?

Most couples spend 45 to 90 minutes once per month. Optional mini check-ins can be 10 minutes.

Make space for each other.
